My name is Tina Dam



Professional
I’m a UX designer with a background in pharma copywriting. I've written promotional content for medications across many therapeutic areas, primarily for healthcare professional audiences. Over time, I’ve come to better understand the power of words and how to tell a story. Now, I want to be able to create a whole experience out of it.

What does that mean? How do I craft a story that is pleasantly engaging for people, from the interactions to the visuals? That is what I’m trying to figure out—all I know is that around us, there are stories everywhere and I want to read them, learn from them, and bring them to life.
