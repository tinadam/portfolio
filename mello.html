mello, a mental health support app


We created a mental health app incorporating both immediate and longer-term support to help further the mission of the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline



Timeline: 2 weeks
Project Type: Group
My Roles: UX research, UX design, front-end development, UI design (secondary)
Primary Tools: Figma, GitHub, Visual Studio Code

Jump to:  Researching  /  Ideating  /  Prototyping and Testing  /  Iterating  /  Reflecting






Researching


background
Sadly, there are a ton of data out there that support why suicide continues to be a public health issue, and why it is critical to care for mental health.

We chose to look more into how we could help The National Suicide Prevention Lifeline, perhaps by developing a mobile app to supplement their current website. They're a leader in crisis services and advancing suicide prevention, but we saw that they currently primarily focus on immediate care and lack involvement in continuous care.




problem statement
How might we provide continuous mental health support to users during and after times of suicidal crisis and emotional distress?
