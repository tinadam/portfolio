background





The Centers for Disease Control and
Prevention
Sadly, there are a ton of data out there that support why suicide continues to be a public health issue, and why it is critical to care for mental health.

We chose to look more into how we could hypothetically work with the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline. They're a leader in crisis services and advancing suicide prevention, and we were wondering if we could develop a mobile app to supplement their current website.




user research plan

problem statement
How might we provide continuous mental health support to users during and after times of suicidal crisis and emotional distress?
